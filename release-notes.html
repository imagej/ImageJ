    • Thanks to Philippe Carl, fixed a bug that caused the Edit>Selection>Restore Selection command to not work as expected after selecting an Roi in the ROI Manager.
    • Thanks to Leon Espinosa, fixed a 1.49k regression in the behavior, when processing stacks, of the Process>Binary>Convert to Mask command the and "Threshold" widget's "Apply" option.
